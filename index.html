Welcome to Cookie Crumbs, dedicated to teaching you the art of cookie baking!If you love the taste and smell of fresh, homemade cookies, then you've come to the right place. Whether you're an experienced baker looking to refine your skills or a complete beginner eager to learn, we have everything you need to create delicious cookies in your own kitchen. Our step-by-step tutorials, helpful tips, and mouthwatering recipes will guide you through the process of making perfect cookies every time. So grab your apron, preheat your oven, and let's get baking!


Before we jump right into it, make sure to prepare all the ingredients listed below.


1 stick of butter
1 cup of sugar
1 cup of brown sugar
1 packet of NESTLE all purpose cream
2 pieces of egg

2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
4 cups of all purpose flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1 teaspoon of fine salt
3 cups of dark chocolate chips

So what are you waiting for? Turn up your oven and lets get baking!

Step 1
Using a spatula, mix butter with white and brown sugar. Mix until light and smooth.

Step 2
Add NESTLE® All Purpose Cream and mix for until well combined. Mix in eggs and vanilla extract mix.

Step 3
Sift flour, baking soda and salt in a bowl. Add to cream mixture and mix. Mix in 3 cups of chocolate chips.

Step 4
Refrigerate dough mixture for 2 hours. After chilling, mold into 50grams each and chill for another 10mins.

Step 5
Pre-heat oven to 350°F for 30 minutes. Grease baking pan then line with aluminum foil. Bake for 10-15 minutes.
